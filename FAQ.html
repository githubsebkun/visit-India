How can I get the Indian Visa?

You can apply for a 50 days Indian Visa from your nearest Indian embassy. You may have to show a few documents required to prove your intension to travel to India. Generally it takes less than a week. Now there is 'Visa on Arrival' facility in all major airports as well. you can Google the information from the Indian Embassy websites

How can I book Air tickets?

If you are traveling through any agency, they should be able to book air tickets for you. or you can use websites like www.makamytrip.com and make a payment Online. Etihad, Emirates, British Airways, Luftansa, AirAsia etc are a few major companies operating India. you can book tickets from their website too.

Which is the best time to travel India?

North India has three major climates. Dec-Feb is the Winter Time 10-12 degrees, Mar-April Mild Summer, May-Jul Summer 35-40 degree. Aug-Sept-Nov Mild showers and Mild Summer Oct-Nov and Feb-April are the best times to visit North India. Oct-April is the best seasons for South India. But still you could expect 35 degrees any day of the year!!

What type of Dresses/sandals I should take?

If you are not traveling in Winter season, any mild cotton dresses would be sufficient. A rain-coat would be handy. If you are traveling during winter, a medium winter dress is only needed. Leather shoes/sandals are not recommended when you travel out a lot as you may face rains. so normal sandals are worth wearing! They are easy buy everywhere in the range of 1000 to 3000 Indian Rupees.

What vaccinations I need to take before my travel?

Please contact you GP/Family Doctor. Generally you can take vaccinations for Chickenpox, H1N1,Flue, Malaria

What are my essential things to carry with?

Your medicines, prescriptions, a set of toilet tissues, wipes, your visa, passport, air-tickets (a photocopy of them kept in a different place), essential phone numbers, an unlocked mobile phone (In India mobile phones come unlocked. Any sim purchased in India goes into any mobile phones generally).

Any information on Airports and Stopover in-between?

Please confirm this with your travel agent or the flight company website. Please check the Internet also.

Is there any thing to take particular caution?

Take caution while dealing with strangers. Do not travel alone very late. Do not disclose your whereabouts to strainers. Do not drink tap water. Take mineral water. Do not eat spicy breakfast if you are not used to. Continental breakfast will be available in most hotels. Call /use only approved taxi preferably arranged by your Hotel.

How is the taxi operating in India?

There is taxi service of Car, Van, Auto-rikshaw and your hotel should be able to arrange this suitably. Please arrange for your travel in India with your agent.

How is the Train travel in India? Do I need to book tickets?

If you are very sure of the travel by train, you can book the train tickets Online http://www.indianrail.gov.in/ .Take a printout of the ticket for your reference during travel. Generally train accommodations are 1st class Air Condition, 2nd class Air Condition, Air Condition Chair Car(Sitting only),1st Class Sleeper(no Air Condition) 2nd Class Sleeper (no Air Condition), General Class (very few coaches and will be congested).

How feasible is the bus travel in India?

Bus tickets from place to place are available at main bus stations. You may not need to book them in advance. Tickets/seats can be booked from the main bus stations

Can I smoke or drink in public places in India?

There is no public smoking or alcohol drinking in India except designated places in your hotels or in private accommodation. You will be penalized if you are caught Public smoking.

Do I have to be cautious about Indian food?

When you order food, ask the restaurant to keep it less spicy. Please do not eat spicy food for your breakfast. Continental or European breakfast will be available in your hotel(make sure you get them during booking of hotel). Please do not eat from street and food displayed on the street. Use reputed restaurants.

How good is the drinking water in India?

Never drink tap water unless it is filtered and boiled enough. If you cannot do this, buy good company mineral water from good shops.

Is Auto-Rikshaws a good way of taxi service in India?

Be cautious when you use Auto-Rikshaws as you may be cheated. Use the taxi provided by your Hotel. Call Police or contact local police station if needed.

How is the driving in India and Can I drive?

Driving in India is generally safe. The roads are very congested and some times potholes are severely damaging. Vehicles travel on the left hand side of the road while driver sits on the right hand side of the vehicle. (Same side as UK, Ireland etc). Your hotel can arrange Cars on rent you need to drive.

How does the Mobile phone work in India?

Mobile phones are generally unlocked. You can purchase Mobile from a mobile shop and sim of any approved companies. You would need your address proof for a SIM card purchase and would take 24-48 hours for activation. In the mean time you may be able to use your own mobile with roaming.

What is the common way of saluting in India?

The common way of saluting in India is shaking hands between man and man and and saying 'Namaste' (with a closed praying hand and a bowing head) between a man and woman. The same is repeated while saying Goodbye as well.

How is Indian Toilets? Is Western type toilets available in India?

Western Type (sitting like in a chair) toilets are available all over India except in some remote villages where Indian style toilets are only (or no toilets at all!!) available. If you are planning remote village visits, make things clear with your agent.

How do I deal with beggars?

Please do not entertain beggars as some of them can attack you. If you like to do good to beggars, contact your nearest church or temple or any of the nearest social institutions who can take your donation and give you receipt.

What are the emergency contact numbers?

Emergency contact numbers In case of Emergency contact the nearest police station. Numbers are Police -100 Ambulance - 102, Fire -101. Chennai Traffic Police 103, Delhi Traffic Police 1095, Kolkata Traffic Police 1073, many states use emergency contact 108.

in case of issues related to embassy or High commissioner involvements contact your nearest embassy. Most embassy are located in New Delhi. The police stations will be able to assist you.

How do I get medical treatments? Is the System of family doctor available?

The system of General Practitioner or Family doctor is not generally available in India. You can see the private practicing doctors at their private clinics. Your Hotel staff or travel guide will be able to guide you effectively in this regard. In case of emergency go to the nearest hospital A&E. most hospitals have A&E open all through the day and night.

How can I exchange my money in EUR/Dollar to Indian money?

You can get Indian money against all major currencies at all airports provided you show your passport. All airports will have bank counters or companies like Thomas cook. Foreign currencies can be spent directly at airports only. You would need Indian Rupee for all your daily transactions out side airport. Keep your money in two separate places for safe keep. You can draw Indian Rupee from ATMs using all international credit cards, but will incur transaction and exchange charges. Your hotel/banks near you also can help you with money transaction. Please make sure you get correct exchange rates. Here is a link to xe.com

How can I detect counterfeit Indian Currency?

Counterfeit notes are a prevailing problem in India. Please familiarize yourself with the Indian Rupee by clicking on the link of the Reserve Bank of India here

Can someone send money to me while I am in India, if i have shortage?

Of course. Money transferring companies like Western Union are popular and you can find them in the nearest post office.
